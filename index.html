Biologist • Illustrator • Science Communicator

First and Foremost: A Biologist

My research focuses on sensory systems, hormones, and neurobiology in an effort to understand the full picture of inter- and intraspecies communication.

By investigating the senses organisms use to recognize mates, and the hormones that influence aggression, cooperation, and parental care, I study how communication shapes — and sometimes breaks down — species boundaries and their ecological communities.

Where Biology Meets Art

An important — and often overlooked — part of being a scientist is sharing discoveries with people outside of your immediate field of research.

Through illustration and visual storytelling, I strive to make complex biological ideas approachable, engaging, and meaningful to people from all backgrounds.
